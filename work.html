Iconic SF

“Iconic SF” represents major tourist destinations in San Francisco with nature. The Gold Gate Bridge is paired with the state’s flower, a California poppy. The Ferry Building where farmers markets and other events take place, is paired with an orange that could be purchased at the market. Lastly, the California Science Museum which represents the plethora of museums and art exhibits SF houses, is paired with a butterfly which can be found within the science center's dome habitat.

Growing With You

“Growing with you” was created to show the blossoming of new love. The image consists of various images of flowers found all around San Francisco. From flowers in the Conservatory of Flowers to flowers found blooming on the streets. Love can and does blossom and grow everywhere. Main image by Kaila Troncoso. Floral pictures by Sara Denham.

SF Architecture

“SF Architecture” simplifies and highlights the architecture of San Francisco. Using only five colors and simple shapes, Sara has shown the complex architecture in a simplified, clean look. The artwork highlights San Franciscos intricate architecture.

New Home

“New Home” depicts Sara’s first few months in San Francisco. Exploring the city and of course capturing the beauty of the famous bridge that connects the bay to its neighbors. The images background lacks color in order to bring the focus to the foreground where the greeny, teal ocean and vibrant red/orange of the bridge can be fully appreciated.

Don't Judge

The eyes are known as the window into people’s souls and thoughts as well as the body part we use to judge and observe others. Here Sara wanted to emphasize the idea that we can use our eyes to not “judge a book by its cover”. The fiery eye contrasts with the colorless background to bring the focus to the eye and the copy that is in line with it.
